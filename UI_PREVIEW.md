## Color Scheme

### Status Colors
- **Live**: Red background (#FEE2E2), Red text (#991B1B)
  - Includes pulsing red dot animation
- **Upcoming**: Blue background (#DBEAFE), Blue text (#1E3A8A)
- **Full Time**: Gray background (#F3F4F6), Gray text (#1F2937)

### UI Colors
- **Primary**: Blue (#1E40AF) - Active states, highlights
- **Background**: Light gray (#F9FAFB) - Page background
- **Cards**: White (#FFFFFF) - Match cards, sidebar
- **Text**: Dark gray (#111827) - Main text
- **Secondary Text**: Medium gray (#6B7280) - Timestamps, venues
- **Border**: Light gray (#E5E7EB) - Card borders, dividers

### Hover Effects
- Cards: Shadow increases on hover
- Buttons: Slight darkening
- Sidebar items: Light gray background (#F9FAFB)

## Typography

### Font Family
- Primary: Inter (sans-serif)
- Fallback: system-ui, -apple-system, sans-serif

### Font Sizes
- Page Title: 30px (text-3xl)
- Section Header: 24px (text-2xl)
- Team Names: 18px (text-lg)
- Scores: 20px (text-xl)
- Body Text: 16px (text-base)
- Small Text: 14px (text-sm)
- Tiny Text: 12px (text-xs)

### Font Weights
- Bold: 700 (titles, team names)
- Semibold: 600 (scores, active items)
- Medium: 500 (labels)
- Normal: 400 (body text)

## Spacing System

- Extra Small: 4px (1 unit)
- Small: 8px (2 units)
- Medium: 16px (4 units)
- Large: 24px (6 units)
- Extra Large: 32px (8 units)
